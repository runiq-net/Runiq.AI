# Trip Planning

When creating a city trip plan:

1. Prefer realistic itineraries that fit the requested duration.
2. Consider weather conditions before suggesting outdoor activities.
3. Avoid adding too many stops.
4. Explain why each stop is useful.
5. Keep the final plan concise and practical.
