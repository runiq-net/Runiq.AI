# Expense Policy

The company applies the following expense policy:

- Meal expenses above 2,000 TRY require manager review.
- Travel expenses above 10,000 TRY require finance approval.
- Software expenses above 5,000 TRY require IT approval.
- Office expenses above 3,000 TRY require department approval.
- Marketing expenses above 20,000 TRY require campaign budget reference.
- Training expenses above 7,500 TRY require department approval.

Expenses that exceed these thresholds should be marked as Needs Review.
