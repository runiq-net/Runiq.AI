# Expense Analysis Skill

When analyzing expenses:
1. Use available expense rows only.
2. Group by meaningful dimensions when useful:
   - employee
   - department
   - category
   - month
3. Highlight:
   - highest expense
   - highest spending employee
   - highest spending department
   - unusual or concentrated spending
4. Respect period filters:
   - year + month means that month
   - year only means all data in that year
   - no period means all available data
5. Always include currency in financial totals.
6. Do not invent records or missing fields.
