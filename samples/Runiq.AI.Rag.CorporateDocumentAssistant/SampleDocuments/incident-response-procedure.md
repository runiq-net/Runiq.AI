# Incident Response Procedure

After discovering a security incident, an employee must first protect people from immediate harm, then report the incident to Security Operations within 30 minutes.

The employee should record the time, affected system, and visible symptoms. If malware or unauthorized access is suspected, disconnect the device from the network without powering it off. Do not delete messages, logs, or files because they may be required as evidence.

Security Operations owns containment, investigation, recovery, and communication. Employees must follow the incident lead's instructions and must not contact external parties unless Communications or Legal authorizes it.
