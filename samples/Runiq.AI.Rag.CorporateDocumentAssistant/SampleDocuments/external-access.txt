External Access Request Procedure

External access is required when a partner, vendor, or employee needs to reach internal systems from outside the corporate network. The requester must identify the system, access duration, business owner, and reason for access.

Temporary access should use the shortest practical duration and must be reviewed by the system owner. Sensitive systems may require security approval and additional monitoring.

When access is no longer required, the requester or system owner must notify IT so the permission can be removed.
