# Information Security Policy

Employees must use multi-factor authentication for company services and must never share passwords or authentication codes. Confidential documents may be stored only in company-approved systems.

Suspected phishing, lost devices, accidental disclosure, or unauthorized account access must be reported to the Security Operations team within 30 minutes. The employee should disconnect an affected device from the network but must not delete files or attempt an independent forensic investigation.

Security Operations can be reached through the security incident portal or the internal emergency number listed on the employee intranet.
