# Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely for up to three days per week. The employee and manager must agree which days are remote so that team coverage and on-site meetings are maintained.

Remote work must be performed from an approved location with a reliable internet connection. Employees must use the company VPN for internal systems and must not allow family members or other third parties to use company devices.

Working remotely does not change normal working hours or availability expectations. A temporary exception beyond three days requires written approval from the employee's department director.
