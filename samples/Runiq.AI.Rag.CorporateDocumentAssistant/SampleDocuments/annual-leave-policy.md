# Annual Leave Policy

Full-time employees receive 20 paid annual-leave days per calendar year. Requests for one or two days should be submitted at least five business days in advance.

Requests for three or more consecutive days should be submitted at least 15 calendar days in advance and require manager approval. Approval depends on team coverage and previously approved leave.

Up to five unused days may be carried into the next calendar year. Carried days expire on March 31 if they are not used.
