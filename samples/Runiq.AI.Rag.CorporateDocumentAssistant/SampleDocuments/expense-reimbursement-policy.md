# Expense Reimbursement Policy

Employees must submit business expenses within 30 calendar days of the transaction. Each claim must include an itemized receipt and a short business purpose.

Any single expense above USD 250 requires manager approval before reimbursement. Air travel, hotel stays, and conference registrations require manager approval regardless of amount.

Personal purchases, minibar charges, traffic fines, and first-class travel are not reimbursable. Finance normally processes an approved claim within ten business days.
