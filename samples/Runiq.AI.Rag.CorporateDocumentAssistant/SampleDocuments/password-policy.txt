Password Policy

Corporate passwords must be changed immediately if an employee suspects credential exposure or receives an IT security notification. Passwords should be unique to the corporate account and must not be reused from personal services.

The recommended password length is at least fourteen characters. Employees should use a passphrase or password manager generated value and must keep multi-factor authentication enabled.

After a password change, employees may need to update credentials in the VPN client, email client, source control tools, and approved mobile applications.
